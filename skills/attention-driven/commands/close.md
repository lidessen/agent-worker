# attention-driven:close

Close durable work surfaces cleanly.

## Goal Initiative

For a goal initiative:

1. Read `goals/GOAL.md`, records, and open STOPs.
2. Draft a retrospective in chat: achieved criteria, missed criteria, major
   path changes, unresolved lessons, and final judgment.
3. Ask the human whether the goal is complete, abandoned, or superseded.
4. After confirmation, mark the goal closed and append a final record entry.

Do not silently rewrite the goal to match the outcome.

## Reframe Concept

For a reframe concept:

1. Decide whether it is adopted, abandoned, or superseded.
2. If adopted, route load-bearing shape into design first.
3. Record why it closed and what future work should remember.
4. Archive or mark the concept closed.

## Blueprint

For a completed blueprint:

1. Confirm Verify checks have fact observations where needed.
2. Strip temporary TODO/State scaffold.
3. Keep follow-ups only if they are real pending claims.
4. Leave the blueprint as a historical record.
